**EiTS** is an **ESP32-S3** gadget that plays **MJPEG** and **MP3** from a **microSD** card, with **one button** gestures, **battery** status, optional **OTA** updates, and Windows **FFmpeg** helper tools.

* **Repository:** [github.com/Nischay2312/EiTS](https://github.com/Nischay2312/EiTS)
